About our school

Dominion school is a small school with around 50 kids from a village near Kaseh. Those are the children of parents, who can't afford to pay for education or don't want to send their kids far away from their home. The teacher (picture on right) is teaching children only for little fee that is used for food during the day.

Volunteer with us!

We would appreciate your help for any period of time. We cannot afford to pay for your accomodation and food, so you would have to pay these necessary costs yourself, but our volunteering programme is otherwise completely free.

We will of course help you with accomodation and we will cook for you as well. You can find the prices below.

1 week volunteering 100 USD
2 weeks volunteering 160 USD
every other week volunteering 80 USD
airport pickup 40 USD

If you will decide to help with our little project, just contact us and we will arrange the airport pickup (please note that there is a fee of 40 USD for the pickup). Or you can decide to travel by yourself, however if you are in Ghana for the first time we do not recommend it.
